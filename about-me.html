HEY THERE!

I'm a legal associate turned UX/UI Designer. As a UX designer I love contributing in the building of our virtual habits and I believe that good UX is good business.

In my career as a legal associate in notary office I have trained myself to be extremely observant of details, proactive, and engaged professional who never stops asking "why". My background has helped me to better solve design problems by keeping users in mind. I know the value of understanding customer needs, and keeping business objectives in mind.

I studied UX design through a project-based program with Brainster Academy, which has given me a holistic view of the entire UX design process and an appreciation for working on projects for companies from ideation to final design. I have spent the past year studying the UX design process, design tools, and psychology's role in design.

I am currently looking for opportunities to grow as a UX/UI Designer.
 Feel free to reach out at a.lalevik@gmail.com or Linkedin. I'd love to hear from you.

BUT WHY UX DESIGN?

I believe that user experience design is at the perfect intersection of creativity and analysis - while there is so much room for creative expression, I love that UX design focuses on the user and that solutions are based on real, human needs.

I love empathizing with people and understanding what motivates them to think and act the way they do, and further using those insights in my designs.

Aside from this, I’m a big fan of playing with photography, skiing, chasing my energetic toddler and traveling.

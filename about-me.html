HEY THERE!

I'm a legal associate turned UX/UI Designer on a mission to improve human experiences through design.

In my career as a legal associate in notary office I have trained myself to be extremely observant of details and to answer all possible if-question. My background has helped me to better solve design problems by keeping users in mind. I know the value of understanding customer needs, and keeping business objectives in mind.

I studied UX design through a project-based program with Brainster Academy, which has given me a holistic view of the entire UX design process and an appreciation for working on projects for real companies from ideation to final design. I have spent the past year studying the UX design process, design tools, and psychology’s role in design.

My journey has been fueled by my insatiable desire to learn and grow. I spend a lot of time adding to my knowledge of UX through books, courses, and online events. I am currently looking for opportunities to grow as a UX/UI Designer.

Feel free to reach out at a.lalevik@gmail.com or Linkedin. I’d love to hear from you.

BUT WHY UX DESIGN?

I believe that user experience design is at the perfect intersection of creativity and analysis - while there is so much room for creative expression, I love that UX design focuses on the user and that solutions are based on real, human needs.

I love empathizing with people and understanding what motivates them to think and act the way they do, and further using those insights in my designs.

Aside from this, I’m a big fan of playing with photography, skiing, chasing my energetic toddler and traveling.
